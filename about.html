About JCAP

Any trading environment is a fast-paced one; we keep the rest casual.

That means casual dress, a friendly feel, and a commitment to work-life balance.

And we live that each and every day.

Our benefits include flexible schedules, company sponsored social activities, and a trust-based (i.e. unlimited) vacation policy.

Trading gives us the ability to live our career dreams, but we realize we had plenty of support and lots of good fortune getting here. Accordingly, we make it a priority to give back so that others can pursue their dreams.
